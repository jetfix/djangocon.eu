Conference Venue

An inspiring view over the old docks of Amsterdam

Pakhuis de Zwijger

{% with "Pakhuis de Zwijger, Piet Heinkade 179, 1019 HC Amsterdam"|urlencode as venue_address %} {% endwith %}

The conference days will be June 6-8

The conference location is an old warehouse, part of the old eastern docks of the port of Amsterdam. The docks are no longer in active use, since the port of Amsterdam relocated to the very outskirts of the city and became heavily industrialized.

Still the area is characterized by an amazing set of warehouses and old machinery. From the conference venue w'll have a lovely view over the water. So if you need room to think because of all the things you've learned at the conference. It's there.

Just wait and see how Amsterdam looks during the sunny month of June!

Sprint venue

Non-stop? Yessir!

de Waag

{% with "Nieuwmarkt 4, 1012 CR Amsterdam"|urlencode as venue_address %} {% endwith %}

The sprint days will be June 9-10

The sprint location is a place called De Waag which is an old castle in the very heart of Amsterdam. It is the old weighing house and is famous for housing the Amsterdam Guild of Surgeons during the 17th century which was painted by Rembrandt van Rijn: The Anatomy Lesson of Dr. Nicolaes Tulp.

Also the location is infamous for being the first to allow paying visitors watch the Surgeons dissect dead human bodies. All in the name of science of course!

The sprints

The sprint location will be open for us 48-hours non stop, meaning literally that we can sprint until we drop. If (when) you drop we have field beds so you can sleep on location. Of course if you prefer the calmth of your hotel, the hotels are all just around the corner.

We will do everything we can to make sure the sprints will be as fun and productive as possible for all participants. This includes:

  1. Running up to the the conference we'll coordinate with the Core Developers to make sure we have stuff to do that's both useful and doable.
  2. At least one of the talks on the main conference days will be dealing with contributing to Django in general and the sprint in particular.
  3. For those who are not familiar with sprints: we'll make sure to ease you into the experience on the actual sprint days.
Sprints are going to be awesome!
