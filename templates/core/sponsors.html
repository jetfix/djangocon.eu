The list of sponsors is definitive

DjangoCon is a community conference, organised entirely by volunteers. However, DjangoCon would not have been possible without financial help from our generous sponsors. To the sponsors we say: Thank you!

Below is a list of all our sponsors, make sure to visit their sites to inform yourself of the various and interesting things they are doing in the Django-sphere. Some of our sponsors are looking for talented programmers, if you're looking for a job, please view the job-opportunities they offer on their site.

If you would like to be a sponsor of DjangoCon Europe next year, you can still email us at sponsors@djangcon.eu.
