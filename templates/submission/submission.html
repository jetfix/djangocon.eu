DjangoCon Europe

Talk submission

Because Djangocon.eu is a community conference, we want to hear from you about the cool new extension to Django you created, the best way to deploy a Django website, how to make apps that are reusable, etc. We're looking for proposals on every aspect of Django. You can assume that all attendees have a basic level of understanding of Django and will understand an in-depth talk.

From past experience we know that not every talk needs the same amount of time, so we're going to create timeslots of different sizes. Please try to estimate how many minutes you can talk about your subject without anyone falling asleep. Of course we can't give any guarantees about the size of the timeslot we will be able to allocate for your talk.

As a community conference we try to keep the price as low as possible and that means we don't have budget to compensate for travel, hotel, etc. or give away a free ticket for every presentation.

Deadlines

Talk submission will close on 15 April 2011. Notifications of acceptance or rejection will be sent before 1 May 2011.

Contact

If you have any further questions or you know someone who can give good presentations about django-related subjects, don't hestitate to contact us at speakers@djangocon.eu.
