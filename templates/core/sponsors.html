Organising a conference is expensive!

There we said it. But its true. A community conference like this needs companies that want to support the conference and make it a truly great happening

Downbelow w've written an open letter to possible sponsors. Please: if you work at, or know of, a company that is using Django or wants to reach out to the greater Django community, show the open letter to the person responsible within your company for sponsoring and have him get in touch through e-mail: sponsors@djangocon.eu.

Open letter

To those interested in becoming a sponsor

Dear possible sponsor,

We are writing you because we hope we can interest you in becoming a sponsor for DjangoCon Europe 2011: the annual European conference on the Django Webframework. Organised this year in Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

Django is Open Source software, like Linux, that allows software engineers to rapidly develop websites easily and without hassles. Using Django allows for smaller teams to get better work done in less time.

The conference

The conference will be held from Monday June 6th until Friday June 10th. Three days will be used for keynotes, presentations and panel discussions at “Pakhuis de Zwijger”.

The last two days are organized at “De Waag”. These days will be used to improve the Django software itself. “De Waag” will be open for 48 hours non-stop, so teams can work aroud the clock.

Sponsoring

DjangoCon attracts several hundreds of smart software engineers from across the globe, both in person and via streaming video. Support the conference and gain a place in their hearts and minds.

Contact

If you are interested in sponsoring DjangoCon Europe please don’t hesitate to contact us at sponsors@djangocon.eu.
