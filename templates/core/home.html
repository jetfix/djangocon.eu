Next Year

DjangoCon Europe 2011 was a wild success, with over 200 attendees, lots of great talks, and extremely active sprints.

Welcome

The European Django community would like to welcome you to the city of Amsterdam for DjangoCon Europe 2011.

Throughout history, Amsterdam has been known for its open-mindedness, tolerance, and trade. We're proud to host the conference this year, and we've been working hard to make this conference even better than the stellar successes of Prague and Berlin.

Theme

Django has matured into a framework with a wealth of applications and techniques created by talented developers like you. The focus of this year’s conference will be on sharing these advanced tools and techniques.
